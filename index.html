HELDORYA Server — Welcome to the Castle of Valdareth

Welcome Adventurers

Enter the majestic world of Heldorya, where the ancient Castle of Valdareth stands tall, guarding secrets and stories untold. Spawn in the grand lobby and prepare to explore lands rich in magic, mystery, and medieval grandeur.

Laws of the Land

  • Respect all players; harassment or hate speech will not be tolerated.
  • No griefing or stealing: those who cause damage may be confined in the castle's dungeons.
  • Cheating, hacking, or exploiting mods is strictly forbidden and will lead to an immediate ban.
  • Keep the chat clean and friendly; use English for clear communication.
  • Always seek permission before building near others.
  • Donations and trading are optional and should never be forced.

Explore the World

Traverse the lively village streets and settle in one of the available homes. The spawn area offers safe havens for new adventurers, with houses available for purchase where you can truly call the land your own.

Venture through the castle’s magnificent halls and descend into the somber dungeons, designed to house those who break the peace. Discover the grandeur of the throne room, the bustling marketplace, and the mystical portals connecting realms.
